]]> We here at B-Sides Orlando are incredibly proud of building our local security community together over the last five years. Due to construction delays at the UCF main campus, B-Sides Orlando Security Conference will be held this year at Valencia College West Campus graciously hosted by the Valencia Tech Club.

The conference will feature one full day with three tracks, including a new speaker track meant to encourage new speakers to gain community exposure. This year we are proud to bring back Sunshine CTF developed by the Collegiate Cyber Defense Club @ UCF (aka Hack@UCF). The lockpick village will return with a new escape room. Professional workshops will also be offered, including “CTF 101” meant to educate and expose beginners to Capture the Flag competitions.

Thank you for being the most awesome community of professionals a conference could ever ask for. If you have any questions, please feel free to drop us a line at info AT bsidesorlando DoT org or @bsidesorl

Tickets are now available for purchase at https://www.eventbrite.com/e/security-b-sides-orlando-2017-tickets-31340208407
